Services

Cleaning services built around real site needs

Focused on practical delivery for contractors: welfare hygiene, safe presentation, plot cleaning and clean handovers.

01

Construction site cleaning

Support for live construction environments where presentation, access routes and day-to-day cleanliness matter.

  • Access routes, stairs and circulation areas
  • Dust reduction and surface wipe-downs
  • General site presentation support
  • Cleaning aligned to site instructions
02

Welfare unit cleaning

Routine or scheduled cleaning for welfare areas used daily by operatives and site teams.

  • Toilets, wash stations and showers
  • Canteens, drying rooms and offices
  • Meeting rooms and welfare touchpoints
  • Consumable refill if provided by site
03

Builders cleans & sparkle cleans

Pre-handover and finishing-stage cleaning for plots, apartments and completed areas.

  • Builders cleans before inspection
  • Final sparkle cleans at handover stage
  • Frames, sills, skirtings and touchpoints
  • Careful finish-aware cleaning approach
